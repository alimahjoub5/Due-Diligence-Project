# SERVICES – CORPORATE / VENDOR DUE DILIGENCE

## **H1: Commercial & Vendor Due Diligence for SMEs**

Know who you are doing business with. Our corporate intelligence services help you assess the reliability and integrity of key vendors, partners, and contractors before you sign on the dotted line.

### **Protect Your Supply Chain**
Working with smaller or lesser-known vendors carries inherent risks. Our SME due diligence services verify the legitimacy of your business partners. We look for:
*   **Legal & Regulatory Red Flags:** Past litigation, bankruptcy filings, or regulatory sanctions.
*   **Reputational Issues:** Negative news, accusations of fraud, or unethical business practices.
*   **Operational Verification:** Confirming physical existence and operational capacity.
*   **Conflict of Interest:** Identifying undisclosed links between your employees and the vendor.

### **Why It Matters**
*   **Prevent Fraud:** Ensure you are paying a legitimate business, not a shell company.
*   **Protect Your Brand:** Avoid association with unethical or scandal-hit partners.
*   **Data Security:** Verify that contractors with access to your systems are trustworthy.
